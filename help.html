Kanban Project Management Tool

What is Kanban?

Kanban gives you and your Team an overview of your current work situation and helps visualizing the Project flow in agile Project-Management.

Summary

Here you can find an overview about your Kanban, amount of tasks, how many tasks which status have and when is next urgent coming up task.

Add Task

  • Create new task
  • Fill the form and set a due Date
  • choose category, choose members to assign the task and choose kind of priority of urgency

After creating task you can find it in the board.

Board

All created tasks will show in board. Each task have four sections, you can move the tasks by drag and drop.
depending on current working status.
You can click on task to open an detail overview, which can be edited as well. It's also possible to add new tasks from this page.
Move a task per Drag and Drop to the trash in order to delete it.

Contacts

Here you find your contacts included a contact-book. View Contact information, edit your already existing contacts or create new contacts.
